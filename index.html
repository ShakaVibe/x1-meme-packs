Step-by-Step

HOW TO BRIDGE

Bridging from Solana to X1 takes just a few minutes using the X1 Warp Bridge. Follow these steps to earn your first pack.

1

Set Up Your X1 Wallet

Add the X1 network to your Phantom or compatible wallet. You'll need to configure the X1 RPC endpoint to interact with the network.

💡 Phantom and Backpack both support X1. Make sure you have at least $100 USDC on Solana ready to bridge.
2

Visit the X1 Warp Bridge

Head to the official X1 Warp Bridge. This is the official Solana-to-X1 bridge. Always verify you are on the official URL before connecting your wallet.

3

Connect Both Wallets

Connect your Solana wallet as the source. The bridge will let you select your X1 wallet as the destination — both can be the same Phantom wallet if it supports X1.

4

Select USDC and Enter Amount

Choose USDC as the token to bridge. Enter your desired amount — minimum $100. Remember: higher amounts unlock rarer pack tiers. Max $5,000 for a Legendary pack.

🎯 Bridge $5,000 in a single transaction to get the 🔥 Legendary Pack with 30x token multiplier.
5

Confirm and Receive Your Pack

Approve the transaction in your wallet. Once the bridge confirms on X1, your meme pack airdrop is sent to your wallet automatically. Check your X1 wallet to see your tokens!
